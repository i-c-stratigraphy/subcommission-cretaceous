# STRATI 2026 – June 28 - July 3, 2026

Participants of the Cretaceous Subcommission Session at STRATI 2026 held in Suzhou (China)
Marine and Non-Marine Cretaceous Stratigraphic Correlation: New Advances and Integrated Stratigraphy for Palaeoenvironmental Reconstruction

A gathering of researchers and members of the International Subcommission on Cretaceous Stratigraphy, highlighting recent advances in integrated stratigraphy and fostering collaboration on the global correlation of marine and non-marine Cretaceous successions.

For additional information visit the congress website at <https://strati2026.org/>
